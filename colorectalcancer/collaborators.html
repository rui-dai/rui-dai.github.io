Study in conjunction with Sahlgrenska University Hospital (Sweden), Mengo University Hospital (Uganda – urban), and Mbarara Regional Referral Hospital (Uganda – rural)

Compare diet, microbiome, and colorectal cancer epidemiology in the three populations (US, Sweden, Uganda)

Study procedures do not require any additional visits to the hospital

Specimen collection includes: oral swab, blood draw (41 mL), stool sample (self-collection), 1-2g tumor tissue, 1-2g healthy mucosa (~10cm from tumor tissue)
